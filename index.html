Discover Local hot Spots

We love traveling, exploring new cities and faraway lands. However, it's not always easy to get good tips on what to do in a city you don't know. This app showcases all our favourite Toronto spots so that we can share our knowledge and love for this city with friends, visitors and curious locals.

The curated lists are complied with a love for the small, the local, the fair, the unusual and the beautiful non-commercial entrepreneurs whose shops we don’t want you to walk by. Discover Toronto’s homemade beers, meet Toronto makers, relax at a cafe, find the perfect vintage piece or try out some bike routes.
